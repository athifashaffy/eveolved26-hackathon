Five Novel Research Questions

Gap 1: HRV coefficient of variation (day-to-day variability) — shown as behavioral biomarker (2025) but never tested against PHQ-9
Gap 2: Critical slowing down in wearable HRV — proven in ecology/climate but never applied to depression physiology
Gap 3: Three-way temporal ordering (HRV x sleep x mood) — never modeled at the daily level
Gap 4: Digital allostatic load from wearables (low HRV + poor sleep + high resting HR) — never validated against PHQ-9
Gap 5: Autonomous biomarker discovery loop — doesn't exist yet

Statistical Rigor

Validation

  • Leave-one-subject-out CV (n=49)
  • No data leakage between subjects
  • Appropriate for small clinical samples

Metrics

  • AUC-ROC (discrimination)
  • Cohen's d (effect size)
  • Bootstrap 95% CI (uncertainty)
  • Permutation p-values (non-parametric)
