The Gap

Depression is gradual — patients drift. By the time they report worsening, they've often dropped out.

Treatment response is assessed every 4–6 weeks via subjective self-report questionnaires.

What's Needed

Objective, continuous tracking of depressive state from wearables — a daily signal that tells clinicians whether treatment is working between appointments.

The Insight: Multi-System Destabilization

Neither modality alone predicts depression. Their simultaneous destabilization does.

Critical slowing down: Systems under stress show rising variability & sluggish recovery (Scheffer 2009; van de Leemput et al. 2014, PNAS).

It's not HOW LOW your HRV is — it's whether HRV AND sleep are destabilizing at the same time.

Regulatory Pathway

Health Canada SaMD Class II

Clinical decision support — not diagnosis

  • Classification: Software as Medical Device (SaMD), Class II — provides clinical information to aid treatment decisions
  • Predicate: Continuous glucose monitors (CGM) established SaMD Class II pathway for wearable-derived clinical signals
  • Data governance: PHIPA-compliant, on-device processing, no raw physiological data leaves the device
  • Bias detection: LOSO-CV ensures no single subject drives the model; demographic stratification in prospective trial
