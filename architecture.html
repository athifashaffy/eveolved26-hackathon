Tests each hypothesis with full statistical framework

  • No data leakage

🏆 Key Findings

The interaction IS the biomarker

  • RMSSD slope × sleep dur autocorr
  • AUC 0.78 | d = 1.11 | p = 0.006
  • RMSSD slope × LF/HF slope
  • AUC 0.74 | d = 0.92 | p = 0.004
  • +28% vs standard HRV (0.61)
